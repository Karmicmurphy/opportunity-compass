# Opportunity Compass Product Spec

## One-line definition

Opportunity Compass is an opportunity translator and bullshit filter for small AI-related money paths.

## Core promise

Find small AI gigs worth touching. Avoid the bullshit.

## What it is

Opportunity Compass helps regular people understand beginner-friendly AI side gigs, remote tasks, prompt work, small freelance jobs, automation requests, and hidden niche opportunities.

It takes messy posts and turns them into plain-English action cards.

## What it is not

- Not a generic job board.
- Not another GPT wrapper.
- Not a scrape-and-repost platform.
- Not a get-rich-quick product.
- Not an auto-apply bot.
- Not a tool for spamming buyers.

## Target users

People who want to try AI-related side work but do not know:

- what to search for,
- what jobs are real,
- what jobs are scams,
- what they can actually do,
- what to charge,
- what message to send,
- or how to turn a confusing request into a simple deliverable.

## Main user problem

The internet is full of AI job noise. Beginners see listings and side-hustle advice, but they still do not know what the work actually means or whether it is realistic.

## Product solution

Opportunity Compass translates opportunities into cards with:

- plain-English explanations,
- beginner fit ratings,
- practical tool suggestions,
- realistic pay/time estimates,
- scam warnings,
- deliverable checklists,
- and suggested first messages.

## Core screens

1. Landing page
2. Intake form
3. Opportunity feed
4. Opportunity detail page
5. Salvage Picks page
6. Daily Top 5 page
7. Saved opportunities page
8. Admin import page
9. Admin review page
10. Pricing page

## Main sections

### Free feed

A limited feed of beginner-friendly AI opportunities with basic explanations and verdicts.

### Salvage Picks

A paid-style section for overlooked, lower-competition, niche opportunities. These are not just job links. They include why the niche is overlooked, how to package the offer, what to charge, and where to find more like it.

### Admin import

A manual curation workflow where an admin can paste a raw job post, gig request, public thread, or notes. The app turns it into a draft opportunity card. The admin edits, approves, rejects, or marks it as risky.

## Tone

Plain. Blunt. Practical. No hype. No fake guru language.

## Safety and trust rules

- Do not promise guaranteed income.
- Do not auto-apply to jobs.
- Do not auto-message buyers.
- Do not scrape private data.
- Do not bypass platform rules.
- Use public-safe sample data in this repo.
- Flag scams and unrealistic claims clearly.
