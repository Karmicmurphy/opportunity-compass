# Opportunity Card Template

Each opportunity should be translated into this structure.

## Card fields

- **Title**
- **Source**
- **Category**
- **Beginner Fit**: High / Medium / Low
- **Verdict**: Touch / Watch / Trash / Scam Risk / Too Advanced
- **Estimated Pay Range**
- **Estimated Time**
- **Tools Needed**
- **Skills Needed**
- **Hidden Buyer Pain**
- **What This Really Means**
- **Why Beginners Miss It**
- **Smallest Paid Offer**
- **Suggested First Message**
- **Questions to Ask**
- **Deliverable Checklist**
- **Red Flags**
- **Green Flags**
- **Original Link Placeholder**
- **Saved Status**
- **Salvage Pick Candidate**: Yes / No

## Verdict definitions

### Touch

Worth trying. The work is understandable, reasonably scoped, and beginner-safe enough with basic tools.

### Watch

Maybe useful, but unclear. Needs more details before touching.

### Trash

Low-value, overcrowded, vague, exploitative, or likely a waste of time.

### Scam Risk

Possible fraud, unpaid labor trap, platform rule issue, fake check/payment risk, credential theft, or suspicious buyer behavior.

### Too Advanced

Real opportunity, but not beginner-safe. Requires advanced development, enterprise experience, regulated knowledge, complex automation, or high liability.

## Example safe card

### Title

Local Review Reply Cleanup

### Source

Sample / fake example

### Category

AI writing and customer service

### Beginner Fit

High

### Verdict

Touch

### Estimated Pay Range

$15-$50 per batch

### Estimated Time

30-60 minutes

### Tools Needed

ChatGPT, Google Docs, spreadsheet or notes app

### Skills Needed

Basic writing, tone matching, customer service sense

### Hidden Buyer Pain

The business owner knows they should reply to reviews, but they do not have time and do not want to sound robotic or rude.

### What This Really Means

Write polite, human-sounding replies to customer reviews in the business's voice.

### Why Beginners Miss It

It does not look like an AI job at first. It looks like small admin work, but AI can help produce drafts fast.

### Smallest Paid Offer

I can write 20 review replies in your brand voice for $25.

### Suggested First Message

I can help with this. Send me 5 sample reviews and I will show the tone I would use before doing the full batch.

### Questions to Ask

- How many reviews need replies?
- Do you want friendly, formal, short, or detailed replies?
- Are there any complaints that need extra caution?

### Deliverable Checklist

- Review list received
- Tone confirmed
- Replies drafted
- Replies checked for names/details
- Final text delivered in a simple document

### Red Flags

- Buyer wants unlimited free samples
- Buyer refuses to define number of replies
- Buyer asks for account login access

### Green Flags

- Clear number of reviews
- Clear tone preference
- Pays per batch

### Salvage Pick Candidate

Yes
