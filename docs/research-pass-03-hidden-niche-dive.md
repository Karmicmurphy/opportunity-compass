# Research Pass 03 - Hidden Niche Dive

Date: 2026-07-02

## Purpose

This pass moves beyond obvious AI job listings and looks for overlooked small-business work that can become Salvage Picks.

The goal is to find useful opportunity artifacts hidden in boring, repeated, practical business tasks.

## Core finding

The best early Salvage Picks are not flashy AI jobs.

They are small business support packages where AI helps a human produce a clear deliverable faster.

## Why this matters

Small business owners often juggle customer service, marketing, bookkeeping, social media, and creative tasks. Many are short on time, unsure what to outsource, and uncertain how to use AI safely or practically.

This creates an opening for small, fixed-scope help.

## Strong hidden niches

### 1. Review replies

Many businesses know reviews matter but do not respond consistently.

Opportunity shape: Draft review replies in the business voice.

Best package: 20 review replies for a fixed price.

### 2. Business profile cleanup notes

Many businesses have incomplete or outdated public profiles.

Opportunity shape: Audit public-facing profile text and suggest cleaner descriptions, services, FAQs, and reply templates.

Best package: One-page profile cleanup audit.

### 3. FAQ drafts

Many businesses answer the same questions repeatedly.

Opportunity shape: Turn repeated customer questions into a clean FAQ.

Best package: 10-20 FAQ answers.

### 4. Menu or service list cleanup

Many local businesses have unclear menus, price sheets, or service lists.

Opportunity shape: Clean and organize business-provided service information.

Best package: One cleaned service list or menu draft.

### 5. Marketplace listing cleanup

Many sellers have weak product descriptions and titles.

Opportunity shape: Rewrite listings for clarity using seller-provided facts.

Best package: 10 listing descriptions.

### 6. PDF to spreadsheet cleanup

Many businesses have useful information trapped in PDFs or old forms.

Opportunity shape: Convert small files into clean spreadsheet rows and columns.

Best package: One small file converted to spreadsheet.

### 7. Social post batching

Many owners know they should post but do not have time to think of content.

Opportunity shape: Turn existing business facts into simple posts.

Best package: 10 posts with captions and image ideas.

### 8. Canva flyer cleanup

Many businesses already have rough flyers or handouts.

Opportunity shape: Make them readable and more professional.

Best package: One flyer cleanup.

### 9. Customer message templates

Many businesses rewrite the same messages repeatedly.

Opportunity shape: Draft reusable templates for appointment reminders, follow-ups, estimates, and general customer replies.

Best package: 5-10 message templates.

### 10. Workflow map before automation

Many businesses want automation but cannot explain the process.

Opportunity shape: Document the workflow before any automation is built.

Best package: One-page workflow map.

## What to avoid

Avoid work that requires:

- account access
- unsupported claims
- specialized professional judgment
- large open-ended scope
- private customer data
- direct publishing without owner review
- guaranteed search ranking or income outcomes

## Product impact

Salvage Picks should be displayed as packages, not job links.

Each Salvage Pick needs:

- buyer pain
- why it is overlooked
- beginner fit
- smallest paid offer
- tools needed
- deliverable checklist
- boundary
- where to find buyers
- what not to promise

## Best first product section

Create a Salvage Picks board with fixed-scope cards such as:

- Review Reply Pack
- FAQ Draft Pack
- Business Profile Cleanup Notes
- Canva Flyer Cleanup
- Social Post Batch
- PDF to Spreadsheet Cleanup
- Workflow Map Before Automation
